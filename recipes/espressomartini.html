Espresso Martini

The espresso martini is a cold caffeinated alcoholic drink made with espresso, coffee liqueur, and vodka. It is not a true martini as it contains neither gin nor vermouth, but is one of many drinks that incorporate the term martini into their names

Igreadiants

  • Espresso
  • Coffee liqueur
  • Vodka
  • Sugar syrup

Steps

  1. Add ice in to the shaker
  2. Pour espresso, coffee liqueur and vodka in to the shaker with ice
  3. Shake it weal
  4. Pour your cocktail in to the martini glass
  5. Decorate it with 3 coffee beans
